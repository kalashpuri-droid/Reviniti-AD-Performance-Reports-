Key Category Insights

Monthly Performance

December Actuals vs Plan showed exceptional performance, with Sales achieving 186% of the target and Orders at 184%.

Ad ROAS stood at 5.49 against a plan of 3.37, demonstrating high efficiency despite a 14% overspend.

Impressions were 57% higher than planned (1.78M vs 1.13M), driving strong top-of-funnel awareness.

Ad Type Performance

Sponsored Products (SP) remain the engine of growth, driving 96.7% of total sales with a robust ROAS of 5.95.

Brand campaigns are highly profitable (ROAS 7.35), converting well at 30.4%.

Generic campaigns consume 44% of spend but yield lower returns (ROAS 2.61), suggesting a need for keyword refinement.

SKU Deep Dive

The 300ml pack acts as the primary volume driver, contributing almost all sales (505K) with 100% availability.

The 400ml variant suffered from critical stock issues (33% availability), leading to negligible sales and poor ROAS (1.0).

Inventory stabilization for the 400ml pack is required to unlock incremental growth.
